Data We Collect via the TikTok API

Our application interacts with the TikTok API to provide its core functionality. When you authenticate your TikTok account, we request permission to access your list of public videos. We use this access for the sole purpose of reading the view count for each video. This data is summed up to calculate the total view count for your account and is displayed back to you within the application.

We do not store your video list, individual view counts, or your TikTok access token on our servers. The calculation is performed on the fly, and the data is discarded immediately after being displayed to you.
